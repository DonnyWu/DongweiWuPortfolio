Protosite brainstorm ideas

Amateur Chef's Website

This website is where users can submit recipes that they either created or is a family recipe. This website's intended audience is people who never cooked before and are looking for somewhere to start. Due to the nature of this website, only beginner to intermediate recipes should be uploaded. There will also be a page for recipe of the month, where it commemorates the most delicious recipe that is also beginner friendly. There will also be an event's page where admins will post events such as contests, meet-ups, etc.

Gardening Tutorials

This website will guide amateur gardeners on how to start gardening. The website will have step by step instructions on what to do depending on specifc needs. There will be a blog section where readers can submit their own tips and ideas. Every month the admin of the website will select the best looking garden and feature it on the garden of the month page, where it also keeps all the previous garden of the month winners.

On-campus phone repair

This is a student run organization where students fix other students' devices on UMass campus. The website is designed for people to market their services to the student body. The website will include a section that displays the services currently being offered, which will include fees and charges. Another page will be a comment page, where it will display students' comments and reviews. A contact information page will include contact information, credentials, and resumes of current employees.
